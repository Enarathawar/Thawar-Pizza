Welcome to Thawar Pizza. A captivating, Italian-inspired, and elegant spot to dine-in in the Dallas FW area. Whether you're going on a romantic date, family reunion, or small gathering, the savoriness of our fast-fired dishes will leave you more than fulfilled on all of your craving desires.
